About Me

I've never been great writing about myself, this will be another attempt! I have a great love of cars, passionate about the sounds they make, and the emotions they create when I get to wring them out on some back-country roads, or track environment. Some of the details of life, you don't get to choose, my mom loves to remind me that my first word was 'car'. I turned this passion into a career which actually led me into Web Development, where this will take me, nobody knows!

Aside from everything cars in my life, I enjoy some mountain biking, snowboarding, and still hit the skatepark from time to time. I enjoy problem solving, and regularly am playing with and testing electronic components.
